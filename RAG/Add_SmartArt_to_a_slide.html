Convert text into SmartArt

  1. Select your text.

  2. Select Home > the Convert to SmartArt icon.

  3. Select the SmartArt you want.

Insert pictures in SmartArt

  1. Select Insert > SmartArt > Picture .

  2. Select the SmartArt you want and select OK .

  3. Select the picture icon and find your image, like From a File or Online Pictures . Microsoft 365 customers can also search Stock Images and From Icons .

  4. Select Insert .
